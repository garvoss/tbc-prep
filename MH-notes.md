## Trash - Rage Winterchill
- wowhead notes on trash: https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/npc=17767/rage-winterchill#comments;mode:n10

## Rage Winterchill
- ice bolt: player takes 4.2k - 5.7k instant frost dmg; 4 second stun & DoT of 2.5k per second for 4 seconds (10k total)
    - ice bolt will 1-shot players, 
    - medallion of the alliance or living action potion
    - mages can counter w/ blink or ice block, BM hunters can bestial wrath, pallies can bubble
- death and decay: 20 yard AoE, lasts 15 secs, players take 15% of Max HP loss per second as shadow dmg
    - if casted on tank, boss will need to be re-positioned

- frost nova: 20 yard range from boss, 10 second immobilize, one-time 2.7k damage
    - dispell
- frost armor: melee get chilled debuff upon hit; -25% attack speed, -50% movement speed
    - free action potion
    - will hinder tank's threat generation -> free action potion and pally's blessing of freedom

- a combo of DnD and Frost Nova is deadly, esp. on tank
- boss should be tanked near jaina
- [ ] 10 minute beserk timer?
- [ ] is major frost protection potion viable?

- strat:
    - prep: medallion of the alliance, living action potion
    - pull boss to jaina ; hunters can MD boss to tank
    - BL as soon as tank establishes threat
    - range spread out at max range so minimal players are affected by DnD
    - PvP trinket out of frost bolt; dispell frost novas; move out of DnD

## Trash - Anetheron
- https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/npc=17808/anetheron#comments

## Anetheron
- carrion swarm: targets random player, cone effect AoE, 4.2k shadow damage, reduces healing done by 75% for 20sec
    - casted every 15 secs
    - healers spread out
    - rogue's wound poison can mitigate some healing (reduces healing effects by 10% for 15 seconds, up to 5 stacks)
        - warrior's mortal wound, hunter's aimed shot
- vampiric aura: boss's melee attacks heals him for 3x damage; infernals within 25 yards also get this buff
    - tank infernals away from boss
- inferno: random target, summons an infernal, 2 second stun; happens about once per minute
    - infernal has immolation aura, 3-4k fire dmg every 2 seconds ; and benefits from vampiric aura
    - inferno is a 3 second cast, players targetted by it can run towards OT
- sleep: periodically puts 3 players to sleep, can't be dispelled or broken out of
    - infernal's immolation or carrion swarm can break this out

- putting on some SR gear can be helpful w/ carrion swarm, esp. with healers not getting healing debuff if swarm is resisted
- 10 minutes enrage
- elixir of demon slaying
- care when dps swaps to infernal, as them being bunched up means bad news if they get hit by carrion swam
    - however, both dungeon guides recommend ignoring the infernals (which makes OT fire-res gear necessary)
- immolation and carrion swarm together can be doozy

- strat:
    - prep: off-tank fire res gear; ideally prot pally (~200 unbuffed), pallies have ranged taunt
    - 1 tank for boss, 1 OT for infernals
    - healers spread to avoid too many getting hit from carrion swarm
        - off heals may be helpful
    - OT picks up infernals, tank infernals away from boss;  can possibly be misdirected
        - the healer assigned to OT can be 70 yards away from boss to not be targetted by swam
        - dps ignores infernals
        - move away from infernals when they land to avoid immolation aura
    - wound poison, mortal strike, aimed shot on boss to mitigate healing
    - BL once tank establishes threat
